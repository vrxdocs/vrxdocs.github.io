Customer Integration

Customer integration is the process of adding a new customer to our system. this process is done so that new customers can place their orders using our system.

Procedure

Here is the step by step procedure of Customer Integration into our system.
  1. Customers who want to order lenses approach our sales team.
  2. We sell our products in 3 different categories
    • Nova
    • VRX
    • Private Label (Customer's own branding)
  3. We send the list of our products and prices to the customer, the price of the product depends on the type of the product and with which branding he wants to buy the product.
  4. If the customer likes the products and their price then we start the customer integration process.
  5. Firstly, customers is asked to send their details to us, so we can start the customer integration process.
  6. After getting the customer details all the customer information is sent to the Accounts Department, the accounts team generates a unique customer code for the customer after verifying all the details.
  7. If the products ordered by the customer already exist in our system, then the Product Integration team will prepare a master excel of all the products ordered by the customer.
    • This master excel contains all the details related to the products ordered by the customers.
  8. If the product ordered by the customer is new and not exist in our system then the Product Integration team along with the Sales Team starts the designing and development of the new product, product integration team create the catalogue of the product and generates the new product code.
  9. The master excel created by the production integration team is sent to the pricing department to add pricing of the products, and also create the price in ERP.
  10. The pricing department sets the pricing of the products according to the customers and products.
  11. After this product order details are sent to 2 departments
    • Production Department
    • IT Department
  12. Production department enters product details into the Lensware software, Lensware software checks all the details and if everything is correct only then production of lens can be started
  13. IT team adds the product details to the ERP system
  14. At last the customer is asked to place a sample order to start the testing process.
  15. After the testing process is over, the customer can normally place orders using our system.

Customer Service

Customer service is the support we offer to our customers — both before and after they buy and use our products — that helps them have an easy, enjoyable experience with our brand. But customer service is more than solving a customer’s problems and closing tickets. customer service means delivering proactive and immediate support to customers anytime on the channel of their choice — phone, email, text, chat, and more.

Purpose

To ensure the needs and expectations of customers are met by the organization and to ensure complete customer satisfaction.

Scope

Provide the customers with the best of service in terms of production and logistics.

  • Process orders and request
  • Deal directly with customers either by phone, email, skype or WhatsApp
  • Respond promptly to customer’s inquiries
  • Handle and resolve customer complaints
  • Obtain and evaluate all relevant information to handle inquiries and complaints
  • Direct requests and unresolved issues to the designated resource / department /person
  • Follow up internally on urgent orders, request, and complaints
  • Prepare Service reports and other MIS reports
  • Providing complete dispatch details to customers
  • Maintain account statements of customers.
  • Working with IT team to automate and streamline work to achieve fast and error free processing.

Procedure

  • The orders received in excel form are processed using standard XL format.
  • The online orders are received in Microsoft Dynamics Axapta in under dedicated Customer Account number (EX/0000310) and are printed in lots through batch printing facility.
  • Once all the jobs are printed and checked/validated, the correct jobs are sent out for next process. The one with incorrect values / error jobs are mailed back to customer for confirmation.
  • After production completion, the ready lenses are dispatched to customer as per the schedule fixed. Invoices are shared with our courier partners and commercial team to prepare the dispatch documents.
  • After dispatch, the quantity dispatched and value is shared with Accounts team, Invoices are generated in system.
  • Once this is done, a system generated packing slip is generated which is shared with customer with other details like Invoice, AWB number in dispatch details. Account statement is also prepared by Customer Service Team

Check Points

  • While processing orders through Excel sheet, the generated orders are thoroughly cross checked before releasing for production.
  • We also maintain a register to track down manually which orders are processed and released, jobs which are non-validated.
  • f any jobs are not entered in the server, customers get online acknowledgement, so that they can rectify and re place the orders. All online orders are also checked by CS for any errors.
  • Urgent orders / Re-order jobs are handed over separately to internal tracking team PPC for fast tracked and early dispatch.
  • All Re-order and pending list is mailed to all concerned team internally for their follow ups and so that measurement can be taken.
  • We make sure any recurring issue is escalated to higher authority for their knowledge and proper action. Re orders and complaints are forwarded at labsupport@vrxlab.com, where a dedicated team works on it to provide solution and close the complaint.
  • Important Information is provided to different shift person through e-mail /phone /WhatsApp group.
  • All sample jobs are processed carefully
  • All orders of a new customer is checked by seniors
  • New product launched are checked thoroughly by seniors , before releasing to prod it is cross checked by LMS team also to see if all technical info is coming correctly.

Traceability

Each Customer needs to have unique Customer Account No created in ERP like EX/0000310 (Bolle Protection) to place the order and for easy tracking .When an ECP places an order with Reference Number of his own, against each reference number of the order from the Customer, a unique OCI No like EXP/917740/15 is created in the ERP system. To maintain uniqueness, the last two digits change in ERP once we reach 9, 99,999.

  • Against OCI Number, a Barcode generates, which help the organization and end users to track and process the orders efficiently and then tracked for the status in Production.
  • We maintain / keep the data at different servers since the inception of ERP from 2003 onwards. The latest server contains data from 2018 onwards.
  • In the job ticket (LMS sheet) we have production barcodes, which is scanned to change the status at different production level to trace the exact location of the order in prod. The Status log helps us to understand whether an order is still in process or dispatched / invoiced.
  • In the job card we have production plan mentioned which indicates through which surfacing stage the order has to move.
  • Online customers can also track real time status of their orders. The updated file is uploaded automatically in “File Transfer Application “which customers can download and see the status & also track the real status of their excel orders via below web link.

Inventory

Inventory refers to the products that are ready to sell, along with the raw materials that are used to produce them. There are three general categories of inventory, including raw materials (any supplies that are used to produce finished products), work-in-progress (WIP), and finished products or those that are ready for sale.

Inventory management allows businesses to minimize inventory costs as they create or receive products on an as-needed basis.

Inventory is divided according to two types of lenses

  • Stock Lens (Finished Lens)
  • RX Lens (Semi Finished Lens)

Stock Lens (Finished Lens)

These lenses are pre-manufactured with a certain base curve, prescription and blank size. The lenses are then coated with anti-glare, anti-scratch or both(As per customer requirement), and packaged for retail.

RX Lens (Semi Finished Lens)

Semi-finished lens is the raw blank lens used to produce the most individualized RX lens according to the customer’s requirement. Different prescription powers request for different semi-finished lens types or base curves, it goes through multiple processes (Surfacing, Coating, Tinting etc) Depending on requirement of particular order.

Purpose

To ensure that adequate control is exercised for receipt, handling, storage and preservation of product.

Scope

  • The procedure covers all the materials required for uncut lens production.
  • Semi Finish and Finish lens Store HOD is responsible for implementation of this procedure.

Order Issue

  • The online orders are received from CS Department (via PPC)
  • Orders are received in Microsoft Dynamics Axapta
  • The appropriate product needs to be issued to the each order.
  • In the first step to issue the order we have to find OCI using OCI number in dynamic Axapta
  • After finding the OCI we have to add the lens details inside the Raw Materials tab
  • After adding the lens details, next step is to issue the order from inventory
  • In the final step the order is issued to the lab

Order Issue Using OPC

  • In Dynamic Axapta inside the Automation Menu, select the Domestic Stock Issue or Export Stock Issue Depending upon the type of order
    • Scan the OCI
    • In this process we dont have to type the Left Eye and Right Eye lens value manually we directly can scan the OPC code of respective lens
    • Scan the OPC code of Right Eye lens
    • Scan the OPC code of Left Eye lens
    • After scanning OPC codes of both Eyes the order is now issued

    FSV Store Order Processing

    We use this procees to issue the stock orderes in the bulk

    • Get orders via Email from CS Department
    • We have created master excel format for taking orders
    • Convert the customer order format to company’s standard format
    • Print out of mail body of the order along with the order sheet is handed over to the picking person
    • Picking person goes to inventory to pick the lenses.
    • Average picking time: One person takes 4 hours to pick 1000 pcs
    • Once the FSV lenses are picked, they are bifurcated as per customer requirement
    • If the lens requires Coating (HC/HMC) and Tinting as per the requirement, the lens is sent to the coating and tinting labs for processing, and after completing these processes lenses are sent for packing
    • According to the requirement if the lens does not require coating and tinting, then the lens is sent for packing
    There are two types of techniques used for packing, according to the customer's requirement;
    • Automatic Packing using Autopacker Machine
    • Manual Packing

    Automatic Packing

    • Average Packing time: One person takes 3 hours to pack 1000 pcs using Autopacker machine, Depending on number of types of lenses contained in a consignment (Colour and Coating) this process takes more or less time (since manual intervention is required while inputting type)
    • In addition, certain customer require different envelopes for different types, so that change also needs to be made manually.

    Manual Packing

    • Average Packing Time: One Person takes 6 hours to pack 1000 pcs
    • Certain customers (lower %) demand manual packing of lenses and this process take double the time of automatic packing.

    Scanning (By Barcode Scanner)

    • Average Scanning time: one person takes 1 hour to scan 1000 pcs
    • This is the maximum efficiency i.e. where the barcodes are just on the top of the envelope and so the entire envelope does not need to be lifter for scanning.

    In Box Arrangement

    • This process is used to arrange the packed lens boxes and it requires an average of 15-20 minutes

    Raw Material Replace

    If there is a need to change the Raw material in any particular order, we have to follow these steps..

    • We have to find the order in Axapta by Scanning the OCI
    • Now we have to select the eye for which the lens needs to be replaced
    • Now we have to click on the Populate button
    • After clicking the Populate button we have to add the values in Right Eye Raw Materials and Left Eye Raw materials fields
    • Now we have to click on the Exchange Raw Material ButtonGroup
    • Now the status of order is changed to "Issued by Inventory"
    • Again Status is changed to Send to Lab and Return to Lab

    Production Loss Booking

    Product Loss means any loss of Product occurring as a damage to Product caused by the failure of the handling, testing or storage of Product.

    Production loss also occurs when the product is not made according to the given requirements. when the product loss happen we have to replace the product with a new product.

    • To issue a new product, we have to scan the OCI to find the particular order in which the material needs to be reissued
    • Now we have to Select Department and select the option from a drop-down menu to provide the reason for the loss of product
    • Now put the material details to issue a new product
    • Now we have to click on the Create Journal button to create a new Journal Number
    • After creating Journal number we have to click on the Labs button and select the option - Return to Lab, after selecting the option a dropdown menu will open, from this dropdown menu we have to choose the specific lab
    • Optware Calculation Sheet (For Rodenstock orders)

    Force Base Calculation

    For some orders when the inventory of any SKU (Supplier) is out of stock, we can use the same SKU of alternative supplier by using force base calculation.

    • Scan OCI
    • Scan OPC Code for both Right Eye and Left Eye
    • Send Job To Lensware
    • Updated lensware is printed on designated printer.

    Dispatch

    Dispatching is a process of managing the movement of product to meet production plans or customer demand. Dispatch team ensure that the correct products are delivered to the correct location at the correct time. It forms the foundation of the overall delivery operation.

    Procedure

    • Dispatch team receives the packed lens from final QC
    • Dispatch team shortlists the packed lens according to customer with required customer information, status of order is changed to “Delivered to customer”.
    • Sends the customer wise delivery report to CS team which contains total quantity and value of product to be dispatched
    • The CS team prepares customs invoice, and sends these details to the logistic department, courier partners and commercial department
    • Customized packaging is done according to different Customers
    • Logistic department receives Customs invoice from CS Team
    • Create Label / Airway bill with different logistic service providers with booking of shipments
    • Send the AWB and pickup request to Logistics provider, Customer service department and commercial department
    • Arrange and check parcels and documents as per check list
    • Vehicle movement (UPS/DHL/Fedex/Aramex/Air Freight)
    • Handover to Forwarding Agent – To do customs clearance/Flight connectivity
    • Shipment Follow up with Forwarders and courier partners till the delivery to customer.

    Process Flow

    Accounts

    The accounting department refers to the division that looks after the preparation of financial statements, maintenance of general ledger, payment of bills, preparation of customer bills, payroll, and more. In other words, they are responsible for managing the overall economic front of the business.

    Procedure

    Accounts department receives the orders details from CS department through Email.

    If the order contains freight and insurance details, we have to create the sales order.

    If the order does not contain freight and insurance details, we do not have to create the sales order.

    Sales Order

    • Sales Orders are created in Microsoft Dynamics Axapta
    • In Dynamic AX, inside the Account Recievable select the "All Sales orders"
    • To create new sales order press the Ctrl + N keys together
    • Now the new sales order creation window will open
    • There are 2 rows in the table, in the first row we add the freight charge in the "Unit Price" Column
    • In 2nd row, we add insurance Charge in the "Unit Price" Column
    • After adding the values, we click on the "Packing slip" button, and the order status is now changed to delivered

    Updating the Number Sequence

    • Number Sequence is to be updated in Microsoft Dynamics Axapta under the dedicated Customer Account number
    • Add the "From Date" and "To Date" as per the delivery date of particular order
    • Click on the "List Order" Button
    • After this click on "Update Order" Button

    Invoice

    • Invoice Details needs to be updated in Microsoft Dynamics Axapta under the dedicated Customer Account number
    • Order status is delivered
    • Add the "From Date" and "To Date" as per the delivery date of particular order
    • Click on the "Ok" Button
    • Now in the OCI list screen, click on the Total button
    • The total invoice amount should be equal to the amount given in the mail by CS department
    • If both values are same, click on "Update Order" Button
    • Now Click on the "Batch" Button
    • Select Yes in Batch processing option, and click on "OK" button
