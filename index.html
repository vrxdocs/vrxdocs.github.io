Lens Production
Lenses are produced by successive stages of fine grinding, polishing, and shaping, and conducting rigorous quality control to ensure that the finished product meets the required specifications.
Quality Control
Quality control is a crucial part of lens production to ensure that the lenses meet the required specifications and standards. QC measures typically involve a series of inspections and tests throughout the manufacturing process.
Tint
Tint is added to lenses to alter the color or to provide additional protection against UV radiation. This process typically occurs during the manufacturing of the lens and involves adding a dye to the lens material.
Coating
Coating refers to the application of thin, transparent layers of material onto the surface of the lens to enhance the performance and durability of the lens.
